About Light Academy

Founded in 1998, Light Academy has grown into one of the most prestigious educational institutions in Kenya. Located in Karen, Nairobi, the school offers the Kenyan 8-4-4 curriculum for Forms 1‚Äì4 in a modern, technology-integrated learning environment.

Our Mission

To nurture morally upright, socially responsible and academically competent individuals.

Our Vision

To inspire hearts and minds, embrace cultural diversity, uphold moral values, and deliver quality education through contemporary, technology-driven approaches.

Academics

Light Academy follows the Kenyan 8-4-4 curriculum with a strong academic focus. Students study a minimum of 7 subjects and sit for the KCSE examinations administered by KNEC.

  • English & Kiswahili
  • Mathematics
  • Biology, Chemistry & Physics
  • Geography & History
  • Business & Computer Studies
  • Religious Education

Facilities

Our modern campus includes 31 classrooms, science and computer laboratories, a library, art and music rooms, an infirmary, and a 500-seat ultra-modern auditorium.

Clubs & Sports

Students participate in clubs and sports that promote teamwork, leadership and creativity.

  • Debate Club
  • STEM Club
  • Drama & Music
  • Journalism
  • Community Service
  • Soccer & Basketball
  • Rugby & Volleyball
  • Swimming
  • Table Tennis
  • Karate & Boxing
